JavaScript Summary

JavaScript (JS) is the energetic web developer's sidekick! It's a versatile, high-speed scripting language that breathes life into web pages.
Here's a quick rundown:

  • Makes pages interactive: JS adds dynamic elements like user input forms, animations, and more. It's what makes websites feel engaging and responsive.
  • Lightweight and adaptable: Unlike bulkier programs, JS runs directly in the browser, making it efficient and compatible across different devices.
  • Beyond the web: JS is expanding its reach! It's now used for server-side development and even creating mobile apps.
